About LRCL

Rooted in 1973. Growing with every generation.

Founded by Chicano law students, La Raza Centro Legal has provided free, high-quality legal representation to the Latino community and low-income immigrant families for over five decades.

Our Mission

To provide high quality, free legal representation.

La Raza Centro Legal's mission is to provide high quality, free legal representation to the Latino community and low-income immigrant families. We advance civil and human rights, ensure access to justice for those impacted by discrimination, and serve as the most trusted legal provider in San Francisco and the Bay Area — representing families primarily in San Francisco and San Mateo counties.

visibility Our Vision
"We envision a world where all peoples are treated with deep dignity and respect and where there are no impediments to justice."

Cultural Competency

Leadership

We are an incubator of future leaders in the legal field — cultivating and mentoring first-generation lawyers, immigrant future lawyers, and aspiring lawyers who are Black, Indigenous, and People of Color.

Inclusion
