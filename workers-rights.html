Workers' Rights

30+ years fighting for low-wage and immigrant workers.

The Workers' Rights Program has focused for over three decades on protecting the rights of low-wage and immigrant workers across San Francisco.

Call (415) 575-3500 Contact Us
What We Do

Protecting workers from exploitation.

payments

Wage Theft & Labor Violations

Representing workers in misclassification, wage and hour, and wage theft cases — ensuring workers receive the pay they've earned.

Misclassification Wage & Hour Wage Theft
report_problem

Discrimination & Harassment

Addressing unlawful employment discrimination, English-only policies, sexual harassment, and retaliation.

policy

Ordinance Enforcement

Advocating for enforcement of SF labor ordinances — minimum wage, paid parental leave, lactation accommodation, family-friendly leave, and fair chance.

groups

Education & Outreach

Know Your Rights workshops, community education, and direct outreach to day laborers, domestic workers, and other high-risk populations.

Who we serve.

Low-wage and immigrant workers across industries — including those most vulnerable to exploitation.
